Vachanamrut

वचनामृतं भगवत्स्वामिनारायणविरचितम्

The Vachanamrut is the essence of all scriptures. It contains the precise words spoken by Bhagwan Swaminarayan, capturing His divine knowledge and teachings for eternal spiritual guidance.

"One who has profound love for God and His Sant considers himself to be brahmarup and offers upāsanā to Purushottam while maintaining a master-servant relationship with Him."

- Vachanamrut Gadhada Pratham 54

"The jiva attains ultimate liberation only when it identifies itself with the brahmarup form and offers bhakti to Purushottam."

- Vachanamrut Gadhada Madhya 13
